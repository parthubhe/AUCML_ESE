Welcome to the ICR[Identifying AgeRelated Conditions] Recognizer Tool

An intelligent tool for exploring potential health conditions based on symptoms and demographic data.

About the Project

This application leverages a machine learning models and GenAI to predict the probability of various age-related diseases. By entering a few key data points, such as symptoms and vital signs, you can receive a list of potential conditions ranked by likelihood.

How It Works
  • Machine Learning Model: At its core,A NeuralNetwork/XGBoost/Random Forest classifier analyzes your input to calculate probabilities for numerous diseases.
  • Data-driven Insights: The model is trained on a comprehensive dataset of patient profiles and symptoms to ensure robust predictions.
  • AI-Powered Diagnostics: We integrate with a local Large Language Model (via Ollama) to provide a context-aware diagnostic summary and recommend potential next steps in a clear, understandable format.
